The visual design of this site captures Hong Kong’s balance between modern vibrancy and cultural depth. I chose a clean, elegant layout using Playfair Display for headings to convey sophistication and Open Sans for body text for readability.

The color palette draws from the city’s night skyline—deep blues and gold accents represent the energy of city lights against the harbor. Images are large and immersive, showcasing Hong Kong’s diverse attractions and cuisine.

Buttons are rounded and warm-colored to encourage user interaction while maintaining harmony with the visual tone. The consistent navigation bar and footer provide intuitive access and a cohesive browsing experience across all pages.

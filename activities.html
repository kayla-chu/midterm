Explore & Experience

From breathtaking skyline views to spiritual escapes and cultural journeys, Hong Kong offers something for every traveler. Below are three must-do experiences that capture the spirit of this dynamic city.

1. Victoria Peak

Ride the historic Peak Tram and ascend to Hong Kong’s most iconic viewpoint. From the top, take in panoramic vistas of the glittering harbor, lush hills, and towering skyscrapers that define the city’s skyline. Whether you visit at sunrise or after dark, The Peak offers one of the most unforgettable sights in Asia.

2. Star Ferry Ride

Hop aboard the classic green-and-white Star Ferry to glide across Victoria Harbour. Operating since 1888, this short journey between Hong Kong Island and Kowloon gives you front-row views of the skyline — especially stunning at sunset when the city lights begin to glow.

3. Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha)

Located on Lantau Island, the 112-foot-tall bronze Tian Tan Buddha is a symbol of peace and harmony. Climb the 268 steps to reach the statue and enjoy sweeping views of the mountains and South China Sea. Nearby, you can explore Po Lin Monastery and the Ngong Ping Village for a complete spiritual retreat.
